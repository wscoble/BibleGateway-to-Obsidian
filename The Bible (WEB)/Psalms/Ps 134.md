# Psalms 134
***





###### 1 

Look! Praise Yahweh, all you servants of Yahweh, who stand by night in Yahweh's house! 



###### 2 

Lift up your hands in the sanctuary. Praise Yahweh! 



###### 3 

May Yahweh bless you from Zion, even he who made heaven and earth.
